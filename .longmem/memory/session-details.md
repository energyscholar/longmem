## Archival Protocol

Sessions get a significance flag:
- **PARADIGM:** Major breakthrough, new direction, corrected assumption, or user flags it. Keep 3-5 line summaries indefinitely.
- **ROUTINE:** Incremental progress, expected execution. Compress to 2-3 lines after archival.

**If unsure, default PARADIGM** — cost of keeping a 5-line block is negligible.

---

## Format

```markdown
### Session N (PARADIGM | ROUTINE, YYYY-MM-DD)
[3-5 line summary for PARADIGM, 2-3 lines for ROUTINE after compression]
```
